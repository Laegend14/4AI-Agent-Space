Common Health Issues & Habits to Avoid

Small changes in daily habits can make a big difference in preventing and managing health conditions.

Filter by:

Diabetes

  • Eating late at night
  • Consuming sugary foods
  • Refined carbohydrates

Late meals and high-sugar diets affect blood sugar control and insulin sensitivity.

Hypertension

  • Using too much salt
  • Processed foods
  • Excessive alcohol

Excess sodium and alcohol increase blood pressure and strain the heart.

Migraine

  • Skipping meals
  • Dehydration
  • Excessive caffeine

Low blood sugar and dehydration can trigger migraine attacks.

Heart Disease

  • Physical inactivity
  • Smoking
  • High-fat diets

Lack of exercise and unhealthy fats raise cholesterol and heart strain.

Gastritis

  • Eating too fast
  • Spicy or acidic foods
  • Frequent NSAID use

These irritate the stomach lining and worsen inflammation.

Acid Reflux (GERD)

  • Lying down after meals
  • Overeating
  • Late-night eating

These increase pressure on the stomach, pushing acid into the esophagus.
